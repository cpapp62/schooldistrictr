In June 2018, the Eastern District Court of Virginia ruled 11 of the House of Delegates districts unconstitutional racial gerrymanders. MGGG published a report evaluating the 2011 Enacted House of Delegates plan as well as several of the proposed alternatives. In April 2019, MGGG published a subsequent report, assessing the potential impacts of a proposed constitutional amendment for redistricting in Virginia, particularly the proposed rules for constraints and priorities in drawing districts.
