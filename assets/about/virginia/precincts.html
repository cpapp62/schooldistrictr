These data were obtained from the Princeton Gerrymandering Project and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The precinct shapefile was compiled and joined to election results by the Princeton Gerrymandering Project. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
